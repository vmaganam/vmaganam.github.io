Introduction

Divine Design (Weddign and Events Coordinator)

Divine Design is a company specializing in the organization and planning of various events, such as weddings, quinceañeras, bar mitzvahs, bridal showers, birthday parties, etc. Our brand offers the best catering services, DJs, entertainers, decorations, coordinators, and planners to make your event the best of the year.

Target audience

The target audience is adults over 21 years old, generally, parents who want to organize parties for their children or engaged couples who want to organize their weddings efficiently with the help of coordinators to guide them throughout the planning of their events until the big day.

The primary tasks are to provide importation and also event planning packages from which the clients will be able to choose which is the planning package or service they need according to their budget. Customers should visit this website because we want people who are looking for this type of service to have a place where it is easy for them to find what they need for their events as opposed to other businesses that to get a quote or just look for the prices of the plans you must make an appointment.
